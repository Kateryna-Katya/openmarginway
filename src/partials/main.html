The Concept

Edge-First Match Strategy
At the beginning, only border tiles are active.
Interior tiles remain dormant — inaccessible.
Your task:
Stabilize the outer margin before expanding inward.
As you clear stuff, more of the board opens up.

How It Works

Core Rules:

  • Only matches that include border tiles are valid.
  • Clearing margin tiles activates adjacent inner layers.
  • Activated tiles allow deeper matches.

If you want to unlock everything inside, you need to be smart about how you play the outer
edges.
Don't just go crazy matching whatever's on the edge - actually plan it out and you'll score way better.
